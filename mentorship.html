Testimony

2019 during the 21 days of prayer and fasting, covenant hour of prayer on a Tuesday while praying Kingdom Advancement prayer I had an encounter with God where I saw His spirit come down at the alter and picked up something from the flow and when I looked I saw Him hold the book Monde’s Diary and He looked into my eyes, clasping the book and released it and it looked like it had exploded into a million particles. Those particles were actually the copies of the book, and suddenly I saw the book cover the whole world and He said, “take it to all the schools around the world” and then He pointed at a wall and when I looked at that wall, there was a screen. On that screen was Bishop David Oyedepo preaching and He said, “take after him” as I wondered how it could reach the whole world the Holy Spirit told me to look at Chapter One, Page One, the second last paragraph says, “Diary, without you the world can never know how it is like for girls like me” then I gave God thanks.

Vision

Monde’s Diary Mentorship Movement’s vision is bring Jesus Christ and His gospel of salvation to primary and secondary learners. To get them saved, baptized and established and to mentor them into high standing members of society.

Objectives

Monde’s Diary Mentorship Movement through the Bible and the book Monde’s Diary will build the spiritual, academic and social lives of young people. This will work through Monde’s Diary Reading Clubs in Primary and Secondary schools and Monde Mentor Me Parties every end of the term

The Monde’s Diary Mentorship Movement will operate on two platforms as a start. There will be weekly one hour sessions in schools called Monde’s Diary Reading Clubs where chaplains will host as many learners as are interested per week in one particular school. Through this the chaplain will come up with a list of pupils who are interested in coming for the Monde Mentor Me Party.

The Monde Mentor Me (Triple M) Party will host every end of the term and with demand from pupils will even hold in the midst of the term. The triple M will hold on a Saturday morning from 09:00hrs to 11:00hrs. Pupils will pay an entrance fee of K50. The triple M will give them a platform to raise their concerns and what they have so far learned from Monde’s Diary, it will also give them Bible based teaching that will build them spiritually, academically and socially and finally while at the triple M they will be able to win prizes as the answer the last five questions at the end of the book

The triple M is also a platform where their talents can be harnessed so through the Monde’s Diary Reading Clubs, so if there are pupils who can do poetry, sing, ‘Rap’ or do sketches/plays the chaplains will help them pick up themes from Monde’s Diary to perform in front of their friends at the triple M and be dubbed as Monde’s Diary Talents.
