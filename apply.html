Join Our Team

The Organization is seeking the services of self-motivated and goal-oriented individuals to fill the positions of one thousand (1000) Chaplains across five Zambian provinces.

Name of Province No. Chaplains
Lusaka Province 500
Copper-belt Province 150
Eastern Province 130
Southern Province 120
Central Province 100

Responsibilities

  1. Conduct reading clubs in schools
  2. Appoint Alpha and Beta Champions to sale the book Monde’s Diary
  3. Raise purchase orders for school so that a consignment of books are delivered
  4. Conduct Monde Mentor Me Parties

Qualifications

  1. Grade 12 certificate with English and Mathematics in the best five subjects
  2. Diploma or Degree in any field will be an added advantage
  3. Qualified primary and secondary teachers are also encouraged to apply
  4. School leavers including grade 12 who completed this year (2022) are encouraged to apply
  5. Applicants should be aged between 18years and 30 years

Remuneration

  1. Upon signing the contract Chaplains will be paid according to performance
  2. Minimum K5000, with good performance K7, 000 and with excellent performance K10, 000

HOW TO APPLY

Pay a non-refundable application fee of K100 which will be applied towards your training and issuance of the Standard Operational Guidelines (SOG) and the book Monde’s Diary.

This payment can be deposited in the FNB account; 62942781891 or to the Airtel mobile number 0973999492. When you make the deposit of K100 put the transaction number or bank receipt number on the application form which you will fill in on the organization website www.mondesdiary.org

Or you can email your name to mondesdiary@gmail.com and an application form will be forwarded to you. You can also request for the application form on the whatsapp platform on 0978412058. Applicants who do not pay the K100 will not be considered for training.

The closing date for applications is the 28th December 2022. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted through all the mediums for the training that will start on 9th January 2023 to 13th January 2023. Applicants that are in Livingstone District can leave their application forms at the Department of Social Welfare with the District Social Welfare Officer, Mr Modern Simabenga. If you are not in Livingstone, please send the application form on the email address above or on the whatsApp line above.
