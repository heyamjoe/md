Quickie Loans

What is a Quickie

Whether you need a salary advance or just a booster our Quickie loans allow you to borrow money based on the current value of a vehicle that you own. Similar to home equity loans, except you’ll use the value of your vehicle as collateral for a short-term loan instead of your house. We do not hold on to your vehicle as collateral, rather, it is the value of your car that acts as collateral. So you keep driving your car!

Benefits of a Quickie

If you need fast cash our Quickie loan is a great solution, that is because it is easier to qualify for than a traditional loan since the car value acts as collateral. Plus, our Quickie loans have lower interest rates than other risky loans like title loans and payday loans.

Requirements

You have to be aged 21 with a registered motor vehicle. We need your ID(NRC or passport or driver's license) and the white book for your vehicle. No need for a guarantor. No need for a credit rating.

The Steps

  1. Come with your vehicle for assessment to determine the current value of your car
  2. Talk to a loan office to agree on a payment plan
  3. Get your money and drive off in your car

Apply for a Quickie

To apply, you’ll need to fill out a short and simple application form online or at our offices and provide details about your car. If approved, you get the money within 15 minutes.
